About Me

Hi! I am Neo Ngwenya, a self-taught FrontEnd web developer. I have always been interested in coding and I find that being a FrontEnd developer helped join my coding and creative abilities together. I pay so much attention to detail which can be both a blessing and a curse in life but I feel like it is an advantage when it comes to this line of work.
